This site provides a gentle(ish) introduction to the use of Bayesian analysis for the design and analysis of impact evaluations with a special focus on what we at IDinsight call "decision-focused evaluations."

We assume familiarity with impact evaluations and how to design and analyze data from impact evaluations using standard frequentist techniques. We also assume basic familiarity with Stata.

Comments, questions, and contributions welcome. For those interested in contributing, please check out our contibuting page.
